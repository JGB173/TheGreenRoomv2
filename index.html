The Green Room

About

The Green Room is a yurt in the country side of Matakana where you are surrounded
by farm land and nature. We can accommodate 3 people can have a kitchen, bathroom
and outdoor area.

Location

The Green Room is in the beautiful area of Matakana. You could walk to Matakana from
the Green Room and on the way you could stop off at the Morris and James where you could
buy some pottery or vist the cafe on your way to the Matakana Market or one of the dinning areas
in Matakana
