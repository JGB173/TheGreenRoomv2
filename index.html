The Green Room

A stylish retreat to rural Matakana to relax in the countryside amongst nature.

About

The stylish retreat is walking distance from Matakana just off the main road on your drive past Matakana.
In a rural setting with a backdrop of established trees and expansive hills, this is a getaway like no other.
This is the camping holiday you have always craved but we’ve thought of everything. We’ve tailor-made a glamping
site for people to take the ultimate break and unwind with features like an outdoor fire, a stone bath under the stars
and the treat of watching movies under canvas.

Come rain or shine the spacious 7m-diameter yurt is a beautiful space to relax in, with every last detail sorted including all your
technology requirements. A generous deck complements the tent but on a rainy day, there is nothing more soothing than feeling cocooned
inside the yurt with the sound of rain on your canvas roof.

Location

The Green Room is at the hub of activity. Walking distance to Matakana and only a 5-minute drive to Omaha for a swim or walk on the beach. If you feel like walking we are right next to the bike path that will take you to Matakana, Omaha or Point Wells.

An easy walk down the road is the Morris and James coffee shop and on Saturdays, you can go to the Matakana Farmers Market where you can stock up on sustainable and local artisan goods. From award-winning coffee to mouth-watering brunch dishes, there’s plenty to sample and savour. Relax by the riverside to live local music, while the kids explore the rustic treehouse and natural playground.

In Matakana, there are several great cafes and restaurants like the Rusty Pelican where you can enjoy pizza and weekly specials are sure to please the whole family, or if you are felling for something fancier you can dine in at the Matakana Market Kitchen. But there are cooking facilities at the green room including a bbq should you want to park up and disconnect from the world for a day or two. The utility building a few steps away from the yurt is connected by a covered walkway and has a modern kitchen and bathroom.
